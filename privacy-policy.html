RangeCraft Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 9, 2025

RangeCraft respects your privacy and complies with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account & Progress Data: Email (if you sign up), gameplay progress, XP, streaks, and settings.
  • Device Data: Basic technical information (device type, OS version) for app functionality and debugging.
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data to improve app performance and features.

We do not collect sensitive personal data. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the training experience (saving your progress, lessons, and achievements).
  • To improve app features and fix issues.
  • To communicate important updates (only if you opt in).

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in Firebase/Firestore. We keep it only as long as necessary to provide the service or until you request deletion.

4. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct or update your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
